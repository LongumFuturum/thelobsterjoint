The Taste of California

The Lobster Joint

Welcome to The Lobster Joint, where we bring the taste of California's coast to your plate. Founded in 2017, we're a modern and welcoming restaurant that serves up the freshest seafood and fish, with a special focus on the king of the sea: Lobster.

We’re passionate about capturing the spirit of California in everything we do, from the fresh ingredients we use to the open and airy atmosphere of our dining area. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or just looking for a delicious meal with friends, our commitment to high quality and exceptional service ensures that your dining experience will be unforgettable.

Our Specialities

Our menu features a range of seafood dishes that showcase the best of California’s coastal cuisine. From raw oysters and clams on the half shell to calamari fries and crab cakes, our appetizers alone are enough to make your mouth water. But it’s our lobster that really steals the show, with options ranging from classic steamed lobster with drawn butter to more inventive dishes like lobster Mac and cheese or lobster rolls with a California twist.

Relax & Enjoy!

To complement your meal, we offer an extensive wine list featuring both local and international wines that pair perfectly with our seafood dishes. We also have craft beers and cocktails made with fresh-squeezed juices and local spirits.

Meet Our Team!

In the kitchen, we have Chef Marco, Sous Chef Rachel, and Line Cook Juan. Chef Marco has been with The Lobster Joint since our opening in 2017. He’s originally from San Francisco and brings his expertise in seafood and California cuisine to our kitchen.

In our dining room, we have our friendly and knowledgeable servers: Maria, James, and Sarah. Maria is our head server and has been with The Lobster Joint for two years. James has a deep knowledge of food and wine and loves to share his expertise with our guests. Sarah is a recent transplant from New York and brings her energy and enthusiasm to our team.
